Estuary Exploration

Venture where the river meets the sea on an exhilarating estuary tour. Feel the thrill as you navigate through the narrow channels and witness the stunning contrast of the freshwater river merging with the saltwater sea.

Beach Excursion

Indulge in a leisurely cruise along the pristine coastline of Poovar. Feel the soft caress of the sea breeze as you soak in the panoramic views of golden sands, swaying palms, and azure waters stretching to the horizon.

Sunset Cruise

Sunset cruise in the most popular activity in Poovar. You can enjoy the boating in the evening and during the sunset time we will take you to the Poovar beach and you can see the sunset from the beach. Usually the boats are parked near the Poovar Estuary where the Neyyar river meets the Arabian sea.
